### Slide 9 — Commercial Model

# Zero Upfront. We Earn on Redemptions.

A commission is applied to the **face value of every voucher redeemed**.

**Example:**
- Voucher face value: **100,000 VND**
- Commission rate: **10%**
- Commission generated: **10,000 VND**

**Split:**
- **Giga Mall keeps 60–70%**
- **NonCash keeps 30–40%**

Giga Mall pays nothing unless real value is created.
NonCash only wins when tenants win.
