# Giga Mall Voucher Powered by NonCash
## Partnership Proposal

**Prepared for:** Giga Mall Leadership  
**Prepared by:** NonCash Solution Team  
**Subject:** A Native Tenant-Growth Partnership Inside the Giga Mall App Ecosystem

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Giga Mall already owns the shopper relationship through **Giga Mall App**. NonCash proposes to activate that relationship as a revenue-generating, tenant-growth engine.

Together we will launch **Giga Mall Voucher Powered by NonCash** — a closed-loop voucher and incentive layer that lives natively inside Giga Mall App. It drives cross-tenant foot traffic, increases outlet sales, and creates a new commission revenue stream for the mall.

**Core principle:** We only earn when vouchers are redeemed and tenants sell more. Giga Mall takes no upfront technology risk.

---

## 2. The Strategic Opportunity

The mall’s most important asset is not the building — it is the **ecosystem of shoppers and tenants**. The mall’s main profit depends on one thing: **tenants doing well enough to renew their leases.**

Today, Giga Mall App collects valuable member data, but that data is often under-monetized. Tenants still run fragmented promotions, and the mall lacks a direct, measurable way to convert foot traffic into tenant revenue.

NonCash changes this by turning Giga Mall App into a **native traffic-and-revenue router** for tenants.

### Why this matters to Giga Mall

| Priority | How Giga Mall Voucher delivers |
|---|---|
| **Tenant sales growth** | Targeted vouchers send ready-to-buy shoppers to specific outlets |
| **Lease renewal power** | Tenants see clear, attributable revenue uplift |
| **New revenue stream** | Commission on every redeemed voucher |
| **Data ownership** | All redemption data feeds back into the mall’s ecosystem |
| **Competitive differentiation** | A true digital mall platform vs. traditional landlord model |

---

## 3. The Native Fit: Built Inside Giga Mall App

This is not a standalone coupon app. It is a native extension of the loyalty ecosystem Giga Mall already operates.

```
Giga Mall App member data
        ↓
Smart segmentation (shoppers, lapsed visitors, F&B fans, etc.)
        ↓
Targeted voucher issued inside Giga Mall App
        ↓
Shopper redeems at tenant POS
        ↓
Transaction data feeds back to mall dashboard
        ↓
Tenant sees ROI → renews lease → mall grows
```

Every step happens inside the mall’s owned environment. Shoppers never leave the Giga Mall ecosystem. Data never flows to a third-party marketplace.

## 4. Proposed Commercial Model: Redemption-Based Revenue Share

To align incentives perfectly, the partnership is based on **voucher redemptions**, not software fees.

### Commission pool

A commission is calculated on the **face value of every voucher redeemed** at a tenant POS.

| Voucher redemption value | Commission rate | Example commission |
|---|---|---|
| 100,000 VND | 10% | 10,000 VND |
| 500,000 VND | 10% | 50,000 VND |

### Split structure

| Party | Share of commission | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| **Giga Mall** | 60–70% | Mall owns the customer relationship and marketing channels |
| **NonCash** | 30–40% | Platform, POS integration, fraud protection, analytics |

> **Example:** A 100,000 VND voucher redeemed at a coffee shop generates 10,000 VND commission. Giga Mall keeps 7,000 VND; NonCash receives 3,000 VND.

This means:
- Giga Mall earns **only when real value is created**.
- NonCash is motivated to maximize **redemption and tenant sales**, not just voucher issuance.
- Tenants benefit because they only pay commission on confirmed incremental sales.

### Optional advanced model

For greater sophistication, commission can be applied only to **incremental tenant sales** attributable to a campaign — measured against a pre-campaign baseline. This removes any doubt about whether the program created new value.

## 6. Pilot Program Outline

### Scope

| Element | Proposal |
|---|---|
| **Duration** | 8–10 weeks |
| **Tenants** | 3 anchor outlets (F&B, fashion, service/beauty) |
| **Campaigns** | 3 joint campaigns co-designed by Giga Mall and NonCash |
| **Setup fee** | Zero during pilot |
| **Revenue share** | Applied to all redeemed vouchers during pilot |
| **Data ownership** | Giga Mall owns shopper and transaction data |

### Example pilot campaign

**Campaign:** “Weekday Lunch Boost”
- **Target:** Office workers who visited the mall in the last 30 days but did not buy F&B
- **Offer:** 50,000 VND voucher for any F&B tenant, valid Monday–Thursday
- **Expected outcome:** Increased weekday F&B traffic, measurable uplift for the tenant

### Success metrics

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Voucher redemption rate | > 20% |
| Incremental tenant sales | +15–25% vs. baseline |
| Cross-tenant visits generated | 500+ per month |
| New Giga Mall App member activations | 1,000+ |
| Tenant satisfaction / intent to renew | Strong positive feedback |
