## 1. Purpose

The Parties wish to collaborate on a pilot deployment of **Giga Mall Voucher Powered by NonCash** (the “Pilot”), a native voucher and incentive execution layer integrated within the Giga Mall App ecosystem. The purpose of the Pilot is to demonstrate that targeted digital vouchers can drive measurable incremental sales for selected tenants and create a new commission revenue stream for Giga Mall.

This MoU is not intended to create a binding legal partnership or joint venture. It sets out the commercial intent, responsibilities, and success criteria for the Pilot. A definitive agreement may be executed upon successful completion of the Pilot.

---

## 2. Background

- Giga Mall operates a shopping mall and owns the **Giga Mall App**, which collects member data and provides customer loyalty services to tenants.
- NonCash provides a voucher management platform with dynamic voucher codes, atomic POS redemption, peer-to-peer transfer, role-based workflows, and API-first POS integration.
- The Parties agree to combine these capabilities to launch a co-branded voucher program under the name **Giga Mall Voucher Powered by NonCash**.

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities

### 4.1 Giga Mall

Giga Mall shall:
- Provide access to the selected Pilot tenants.
- Integrate or enable access to Giga Mall App member data as required for campaign targeting.
- Promote the Pilot campaigns through Giga Mall-owned channels, including but not limited to:
  - Giga Mall App push notifications
  - In-mall signage and collateral
  - Social media and digital marketing channels
  - Tenant coordination and communication
- Provide baseline sales data for the Pilot tenants for measurement purposes.
- Designate a Pilot lead to coordinate with NonCash.

### 4.2 NonCash

NonCash shall:
- Provide the NonCash voucher platform and related technology for the Pilot.
- Support POS integration or provide a redemption mechanism for each Pilot tenant.
- Co-design the first **[NUMBER, e.g., 3]** campaigns with Giga Mall.
- Provide creative assets, QR codes, and campaign templates as needed.
- Provide training for tenant staff on voucher redemption procedures.
- Provide a real-time dashboard and periodic reports on voucher issuance, redemption, and estimated tenant uplift.
- Provide technical support during the Pilot period.

### 4.3 Pilot Tenants

Each Pilot tenant shall:
- Accept and redeem Giga Mall Vouchers in accordance with the agreed mechanics.
- Train relevant staff on voucher acceptance and redemption.
- Provide accurate sales and redemption data as reasonably requested.
- Display provided promotional materials in-store where applicable.

### 5.1 No Upfront Fees
During the Pilot, Giga Mall shall pay **no setup fees, license fees, or platform fees** to NonCash.

### 5.2 Revenue Share on Redemptions
A commission shall be calculated on the **face value of each voucher redeemed** at a Pilot tenant POS during the Pilot period.

| Item | Rate / Split |
|---|---|
| Commission rate on redeemed voucher face value | **[X]%** |
| Giga Mall share of commission | **[Y]%** |
| NonCash share of commission | **[Z]%** |

**Example calculation:** If a voucher with a face value of 100,000 VND is redeemed and the commission rate is 10%, the total commission is 10,000 VND. Giga Mall receives [Y]% and NonCash receives [Z]% of that amount.

### 5.3 Payment Terms
- NonCash shall provide a monthly redemption report to Giga Mall within **[NUMBER]** business days after the end of each calendar month.
- Giga Mall shall remit NonCash’s share of the commission within **[NUMBER]** days of receipt of the report.
- Any disputes regarding the report must be raised in writing within **[NUMBER]** days of receipt.

### 5.4 Optional: Incremental-Sales Model
If the Parties agree, commission may be applied only to **incremental tenant sales** attributable to the Pilot campaigns, calculated against an agreed pre-Pilot baseline. The methodology for calculating incremental sales shall be documented in an appendix.
